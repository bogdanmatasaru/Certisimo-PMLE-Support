Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

Go to the Account tab, tap Settings, and select "Reset Progress". This will clear all your statistics and session history.

Is this app affiliated with Google?

No, Certisimo PMLE is an independent study resource created to help you prepare for the GCP Professional Machine Learning Engineer certification. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Google LLC.

How many questions are included?

The app includes 234 practice questions across 4 test sets, covering all exam domains including ML pipelines, Vertex AI, BigQuery ML, and model deployment.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All questions and explanations are available offline. You can study anywhere without an internet connection.

What topics are covered?

Machine learning fundamentals, ML pipelines, Vertex AI, TensorFlow, BigQuery ML, AutoML, model training, hyperparameter tuning, model deployment, MLOps, and all other exam domains.

How do Training and Test modes differ?

Training mode gives you instant feedback after each question. Test mode simulates the real exam with a timer and shows results only at the end.
